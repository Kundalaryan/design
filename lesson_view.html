Anatomy of a Safe Prompt

Learn how to instruct LLMs to process information without supplying them with raw confidential data.

Key Takeaways

  • Always assume input might be used for training context.
  • Replace names and IDs with abstract placeholders (e.g. [CLIENT_A]).
  • Ask the model to generate the template, you fill in the sensitive data locally.

Step 4 of 6 — Cheatsheet

Data Handling Rules

A quick reference guide for your daily workflow.

DO

  • Use approved Enterprise LLM instances when handling internal documents.
  • Obfuscate PII and financial numbers before pasting into any external tool.
  • Ask AI to generate structural templates, then fill in sensitive data manually.
  • Fact-check numerical outputs against the source document.

DON'T

  • Paste confidential client proposals into public consumer AI tools.
  • Upload proprietary source code without explicit CTO department approval.
  • Rely on AI to accurately summarize legal liability clauses.
